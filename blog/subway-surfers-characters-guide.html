Subway Surfers Characters Guide

Subway Surfers features a diverse cast of characters that players can collect and play as while dodging trains and collecting coins. From the original surfer Jake to the newest addition Trixie with her magical abilities, each character brings their own style to the endless runner experience.

This complete guide covers everything you need to know about Subway Surfers characters – who they are, how to unlock them, their special abilities, and answers to the most frequently asked questions about the characters in this popular game.

Core Subway Surfers Characters

Jake

Age: 16 years
Cost: Default (Free)
Special: The original subway surfer

Jake is the default character and the original subway surfer. His iconic look with the orange hoodie and blue pants makes him instantly recognizable.

Tricky

Age: 15 years
Cost: 3,000 coins
Special: The brains of the Surfers

Tricky is a perfectionist and the brains behind many of the crew's adventures. With her red hair and headphones, she's one of the most popular characters.

Yutani

Age: 14 years
Cost: 30 keys
Special: Tech genius & science obsessed

Yutani is the tech genius who thinks she's not from this world. Her alien-inspired design and futuristic gadgets make her stand out.

Lucy

Age: 17 years
Cost: 40 keys
Special: Resident punk

Lucy is the resident punk who loves giving the guard a hard time. Her edgy style and rebellious attitude make her a fan favorite.

Tagbot

Age: Unknown
Cost: 50 keys or special events
Special: First robot character

Tagbot is the first robot character in Subway Surfers. His mechanical design and unique animations add a sci-fi element to the game.

Ninja

Age: 19 years
Cost: 60 keys
Special: Martial arts master

Ninja brings martial arts skills to the subway. His agile movements and sleek black outfit make him perfect for stealthy subway runs.

Note: Over 150 characters are available in Subway Surfers! We've highlighted just the core characters above. The game regularly adds new characters with each world tour update.

How to Unlock Characters in Subway Surfers

Using Coins

Many characters can be unlocked using coins that you collect during your runs:

  • Collect coins by running through them during gameplay
  • Complete missions to earn bonus coins
  • Watch reward videos for extra coins
  • Characters typically cost between 3,000 to 50,000 coins

Using Keys

Premium characters usually require keys to unlock:

  • Collect keys from Mystery Boxes during runs
  • Complete daily and weekly challenges
  • Finish award sets for key rewards
  • Premium characters typically cost between 30 to 180 keys

Limited-Time Events

Some characters are only available during special events:

  • World Tour limited characters appear when visiting specific cities
  • Holiday special characters during seasonal events
  • Collaboration characters from crossover events with other games/media
  • These characters may return in future events if you miss them

How to Change Your Character in Subway Surfers

1

Open Character Selection

Tap on the character icon at the top left corner of the main menu screen.

2

Browse Characters

Swipe left or right to browse through all available characters in your collection.

3

Select Your Character

Tap on any unlocked character to select them. If locked, you'll see the unlock requirements.

Pro Tip:

You can also customize your character's outfit if they have multiple outfits available. Just tap on the outfit icon below the character to view available options.

Character Abilities & Special Features

While most characters in Subway Surfers share the same basic abilities, some special characters have unique traits that can enhance your gameplay experience:

Score Multipliers

Some special event characters come with temporary score multipliers, giving you higher scores during runs for a limited time after unlocking.

Coin Bonuses

Certain characters collect more coins during runs or have special animations when collecting coins.

Special Animations

Many characters have unique running styles, victory poses, and special animations that make your gameplay more visually interesting.

Hoverboard Synergies

Some characters work especially well with specific hoverboards, creating special visual effects or minor gameplay advantages.

Trixie's Magic

As the newest character, Trixie has magical abilities that create special effects during gameplay and can provide occasional power-up bonuses.

Frequently Asked Questions About Subway Surfers Characters

How many characters are there in Subway Surfers?

As of April 2025, there are over 150 characters in Subway Surfers. The game regularly adds new characters with each world tour update, including limited-time special characters. The core cast includes Jake, Tricky, Fresh, Yutani, Trixie, and many more.

How old are the Subway Surfers characters?

Most Subway Surfers characters are teenagers between 14-19 years old. Jake is 16, Tricky is 15, Fresh is 17, and Yutani is 14. The newest character Trixie is 18 years old. Some special characters like King and Tagbot don't have specified ages.

Do different characters have different abilities?

In terms of core gameplay mechanics, all characters run, jump, and slide at the same speed. However, some special characters have temporary score boosts, unique animations, or visual effects. Trixie is the first character to have special magical abilities that can affect gameplay.

Are some characters faster than others?

All characters in Subway Surfers have the same base running speed. The gameplay differences come from hoverboards and power-ups rather than the characters themselves. This keeps the game balanced regardless of which character you choose.

Which is the rarest character in Subway Surfers?

The rarest characters are typically those from limited special events or collaborations that haven't been repeated. Some characters from early world tours that haven't returned are also considered rare. The game occasionally brings back rare characters during anniversary events.

Can I play as Trixie right away?

Trixie needs to be unlocked before you can play as her. You can unlock her using 95 keys or through special events. Once unlocked, you can switch to her at any time from the character selection screen.
