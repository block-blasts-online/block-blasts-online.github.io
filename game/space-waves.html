About Space Waves

Space Waves is a fun game where you guide an arrow through obstacles to reach the end. There are 33 levels, and you can pick any level to play. Each level has a difficulty rating, so you can choose how challenging you want it to be.

The game is simple to play but hard to master. You control the arrow with just one button, making it easy to pick up and play. The goal is to avoid walls, rotating cogs, and thorns while navigating through the levels.

Space Waves is available on both desktop and mobile devices, so you can play it anywhere. The game is designed to test your reflexes and skill, making it a great choice for players who enjoy challenging arcade games.

How to Play Space Waves

In each level, your goal is to reach the end without hitting walls, spinning wheels, or sharp objects. Easy levels (green) have more space and fewer obstacles. Harder levels have tighter spaces and more challenges.

You can start with any level, even the hardest, to test your skills and quick reactions.

The game is designed to be played with one button. You can use the mouse or keyboard to control the arrow. Hold the left mouse button or press W/Spacebar to go up, and release to go down.

  • Move in a zigzag to avoid obstacles and keep control
  • Start with easy levels to practice
  • Time your moves carefully instead of rushing
  • Keep a steady rhythm when moving up and down
  • Begin with green levels and work your way up
