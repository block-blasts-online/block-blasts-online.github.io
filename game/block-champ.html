About Block Champ

Block Champ is a 10x10-style Tetris game with a few unique twists and turns. Create horizontal and vertical rows of blocks to clear them from the game and increase your score.

Unlike traditional Tetris where blocks fall from the top, in Block Champ you place the pieces yourself anywhere on the board. You can clear rows both horizontally and vertically, similar to 10x10 puzzle games. The game features much larger blocks than standard Tetris, requiring careful consideration of available space.

What makes Block Champ truly special are the lightning bolts and ice blocks. Lightning bolts activate when two align, clearing a row without having to fill all spaces. Ice blocks reproduce a square in the same location after a row is cleared, adding an extra layer of strategy.

Can you master the unique mechanics of Block Champ and achieve the highest score possible?

How to Play Block Champ

Basic Rules

Block Champ is similar to Tetris but completely different. At the beginning of the game, you can choose whether to play with multicolored blocks or one single color.

Instead of trying to fit blocks in as they fall to make horizontal rows, you place the pieces yourself anywhere on the board. You can clear rows both horizontally and vertically, like in 10x10.

There are also several much larger blocks than in Tetris, so you have to consider the available space. If there is no space for a certain block, it will be greyed out until you make space for it.

Unique Twists

The extra twists in Block Champ are the lightning bolts and ice blocks. Lightning bolts are activated when two align, clearing a row without having to fill all the spaces. The ice blocks reproduce a square in the same location after a row is cleared.

Endgame

Once you can no longer fit the remaining pieces on the board, the game ends, and you get your final score to outdo next time.

Controls

Drag the left mouse button to move the pieces.

FAQ

What can I do to make Block Champ easier?

You can select the option to play with a single color on the menu screen. This will help you learn more before moving into the multicolored game.

How do you rotate the pieces in Block Champ?

There is no way to rotate the pieces in Block Champ, which is what makes the game challenging.

How do you win at Block Champ?

The key to winning at Block Champ is to clear as many lines as possible. Clear more blocks at once, and you will get even more points, taking you toward success.
